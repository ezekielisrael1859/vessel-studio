Overview

Vinyasa Flow is a dynamic, breath-linked practice that moves continuously from one posture to the next. Each class builds heat progressively, developing strength, mobility, and mental focus through structured sequencing.

This is not passive stretching — it's a moving discipline. Expect sweat, control, and a clear beginning-to-end arc built around the breath.

What to Expect

  • 60-minute class, all levels welcome
  • Warm-up, sun salutations, standing sequence, peak pose work, cool down
  • Modifications offered throughout — no experience required
  • Mats and props provided

Benefits

  • Builds full-body strength and mobility
  • Improves breath control and focus under exertion
  • Develops balance and joint stability
  • Elevated heart rate — functions as cardiovascular work
