Overview

Strength & Conditioning is built on compound movement — squats, presses, pulls, hinges — trained with load and structured progression. This is coached strength work, not a random circuit.

Every session follows a clear arc: prime, load, work, recover. Progression is tracked, not guessed at.

What to Expect

  • 60-minute class, beginner to advanced tracks
  • Movement prep, primary lift, accessory work, conditioning finisher
  • Coached form on every rep — small class sizes
  • Equipment provided; barbell, dumbbell, and kettlebell work

Benefits

  • Builds real, measurable strength over time
  • Improves bone density and joint resilience
  • Raises work capacity and conditioning
  • Structured progression — you always know what's next
