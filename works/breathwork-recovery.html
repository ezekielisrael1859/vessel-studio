Overview

Breathwork & Recovery is guided, controlled breath practice paired with restorative movement. This session brings the nervous system back down after hard training — recovery treated as deliberate work, not an afterthought.

Sessions are quiet and structured, led by cueing rather than flow. The goal is measurable calm, not passive rest.

What to Expect

  • 45-minute session, all levels
  • Guided breath patterns, supported stretching, stillness work
  • Low light, minimal equipment — mats and bolsters provided
  • Best paired after Vinyasa Flow or Strength & Conditioning

Benefits

  • Lowers resting heart rate and cortisol response
  • Speeds recovery between hard training sessions
  • Improves sleep quality and mental clarity
  • Builds long-term stress resilience
